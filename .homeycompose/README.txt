Control your Tasmota-flashed devices directly from Homey using MQTT. Lights, switches, sensors, shutters, and fans are automatically discovered — no manual configuration needed. Requires Tasmota v9.2 or later with MQTT enabled.

Simply connect to your MQTT broker, and every Tasmota device on your network appears in the pairing list. Full support for dimming, color control, energy monitoring, temperature readings, shutter positioning, and fan speed control, all synced in real-time.
